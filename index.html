01
The Gap

Every production on earth.
Same broken infrastructure.

From Bollywood to Nollywood to Hollywood — every production requiring a script and a crew runs on the same patchwork of spreadsheets, PDF call sheets, and group chats nobody designed.

Before
Script to Set Gap
2–3 days manual

A professional AD spends days breaking down a script by hand. Every character, prop, location, and costume catalogued line by line. Permits researched across jurisdictions. Crew assembled via email chains. First-time creators hit this wall and stop.

During
On-Set Chaos
6–7 overlapping chats

Information lands in the wrong group. A 30-person crew across overlapping iMessage and WhatsApp groups — none designed, all noisy. Nobody knows if a critical message was read. Nothing is logged. Nothing can be proven.

After
No Professional Record
50M workers, zero infrastructure

There has never been a verified, portable professional identity for production workers. A camera operator might work 15 productions in a year. Fifty million workers globally. Zero verified career infrastructure.
